## Design Principles Applied

### 1. Organic Growth First
- Content marketing and SEO before paid ads
- Build community authentically
- Focus on word-of-mouth and referrals

### 2. Paid Ads After PMF
- All paid acquisition (Google Ads, Facebook, LinkedIn) scheduled for Sprint 18+
- Only spend on ads after validating Product-Market Fit
- Test small, scale what works

### 3. Build in Public
- Share metrics transparently (MRR, users, growth)
- Document journey on Twitter, Indie Hackers
- Engage authentically with startup community
- Learn in public, help others

### 4. Data-Driven Decisions
- Every channel has clear metrics and KPIs
- A/B testing framework for optimization
- Monthly performance reports and ROI analysis
- Iterate based on data, not assumptions

### 5. Cost-Effective & Bootstrap Mentality
- Start with free/cheap channels (content, social, community)
- Leverage community for growth (ambassadors, referrals, UGC)
- Focus on high-ROI activities
- Outsource selectively (freelance writers, designers)
